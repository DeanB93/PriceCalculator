This Solution consists of the following classes:


Product: Encapsulates the name and price of items in the store
Shopping Cart: Contains lists of products in a user's basket
Discount: Contains information regarding the possible promotions, including the items involved, and the discount amount.
TotalCalculator: Provides a subtotal and total after discounts have been appplied.

Solution assumes GBP (£) is used, and discounts have only '1' discounted item in the deal.
